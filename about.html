The Story Behind It

MyOtherMe started as a simple "what if." We all have moments where we wonder what our lives would look like if we had made different choices — moved to a different city, picked a different career, said yes when we said no.

This project takes that curiosity and turns it into something you can actually see. Type your name and what you do, and you will get a glimpse into a parallel life — one that is entirely fictional but strangely personal. A different city, a different career, a different story. Same you.

It was built purely for fun as a creative experiment. No data is collected, no accounts are needed, and everything runs right in your browser. Nothing is stored, nothing is tracked. Just a moment of imagination.

Created by Ruben Cespedes — a designer who loves building things that make people feel something, even if just for a few seconds.
